Every Day Carry

The breif philosophy of Ever Day Carry aka EDC. Have you ever been in a situation where you've need a small tool to open something, a pen to write with, a piece of tape to temporarily hold something together, or even just a spare phone charger? All these inconvenient situations have a simple solution to prevent future headaches. Having a small deicated pouch or bad to these kinds of things can be the difference between pulling out what little hair we have left or just being prepared by having the means to remedy this small issue and moving on to something more important efficiantly. That is the way of Edc. Just being moderatly prepared for whatever life throws at you. Be safe, be kind, be prepared. Thank you.
